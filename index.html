About the App

CalisthenicsPro is a mobile fitness app that delivers personalized, skill-based bodyweight training programs. Whether you're a beginner or advanced athlete, our app helps you build strength, mobility, and endurance without any equipment.

Key Features

  • Structured skill progressions
  • Gamified challenges and leaderboards
  • Virtual coaching and form correction
  • Wearable tech integration
  • Community forums and support
